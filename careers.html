Ebani Advertising was founded in 2003, with its core focus lying in connecting its clients products/service with people. It’s this singular purpose that has helped Ebani deliver some path defining projects. Infinite Possibilities. Tactful acumen grants us mastery in today's business battlefield. Every person on our team has an unfathomable craving for learning to excel. It's this deep-rooted hunger that gives our clients the clear edge over competitors. We deep dive to get to the bottom so that our clients experience the rise in their business outcomes.

Our rainbow of work opportunities encompasses Branding, PR, Media Planning & Networking, Commercial & Documentary Film Making, Outdoor Advertising, BTL, Digital Marketing and a constant appraisal of adopting new media interventions. The engagements that Ebani dispenses have been utilised by a plethora of best in the market businesses such as GE, DELL, GENPACT, SUTHERLAND, UNICEF, UNDP, PERNORD RICARD, BACARDI, ICICI BANK, RAJIV GANDHI INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT, and many more. The relationships we make are always long lasting for the value we deliver is continuous.

We are a team of passionate members who believe in delivering customer delight while unceasingly enlightening ourselves.
